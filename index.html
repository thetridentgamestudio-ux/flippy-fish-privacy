Privacy Policy

Flippy Fish  ·  Trident Game Studio  ·  Last updated: May 2026

📋 Overview

Trident Game Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Flippy Fish mobile game (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you play Flippy Fish on Android devices.

By playing Flippy Fish, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the Game:

  • Username — the display name you choose when you first open the Game. This is stored on our servers and displayed on the global leaderboard.
  • Game scores — your scores are saved to a global leaderboard so you can compete with other players.
  • Device information — collected automatically by our third-party services (see below), including device type, operating system version, and general location (country/region).
  • Advertising identifiers — your Android Advertising ID, used to serve relevant advertisements.
We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

🔧 Third-Party Services

Flippy Fish uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Google Firebase (Realtime Database)

We use Firebase to store usernames and leaderboard scores. Firebase is operated by Google LLC.

  • Firebase Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

We display advertisements in the Game using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data about you to serve personalised advertisements.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Data Collection

Unity Technologies

The Game is built with Unity. Unity may collect certain technical and analytics data.

  • Unity Privacy Policy

📢 Advertising

Flippy Fish displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads help us keep the Game free to play. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show you personalised ads based on your interests.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your Android device settings: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

We never sell your personal data to advertisers. Ad personalisation is handled entirely by Google AdMob.

🏆 Leaderboard & Username

When you enter a username in Flippy Fish, that username and your associated scores are stored on our Firebase database and displayed publicly on the in-game leaderboard.

Please do not use your real name, email address, or any personally identifying information as your username. Choose a display name or nickname.

If you would like your username and scores removed from our leaderboard, please contact us at the email address below.

👶 Children's Privacy

Flippy Fish is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Parents and guardians can contact us at any time to request deletion of their child's data.

🔒 Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information stored in our Firebase database. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Your game scores and username are stored using Google Firebase, which employs industry-standard security measures.

🌍 Data Retention

We retain leaderboard scores and usernames for as long as the Game is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Local game data (coins, skin preferences, best score) is stored on your device using Android's PlayerPrefs system and can be cleared by uninstalling the Game.

✏️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your username and scores from our leaderboard
  • Opt out of personalised advertising through your device settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: thetridentgamestudio@gmail.com
